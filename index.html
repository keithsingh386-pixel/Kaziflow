Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for the Kenyan creative economy — fast, offline-friendly, and designed to match how you actually work.

Smart Task Engine

Project Dashboards

Visual kanban boards and timelines for every client project. See everything at a glance without switching apps.
